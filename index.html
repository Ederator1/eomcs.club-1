Welcome to Earl of March CS!

The official home of EoM Computer Science.

If you're new, no worries! We will be teaching the basics of computer science at our weekly club meetings (Thursday), as well as prepare for upcoming competitions.

For seasoned veterans, feel free to refine your programming skills by joining one of our upcoming events. Our events include online grading, so you can recieve feedback and results on your code submissions in real-time.

On this site, you can find useful resources, as well as view upcoming events.
